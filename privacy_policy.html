Privacy Policy for Canterbury Mosque Prayer Times Extension

Information Collection and Use

This extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Prayer times fetched from canterburymosque.co.uk
  • Your mute preferences for prayer notifications
  • The last time the azan was played for each prayer

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's localStorage API. No data is sent to any external servers except for fetching prayer times from canterburymosque.co.uk.

Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your prayer time preferences locally
  • Alarms: To schedule daily prayer time updates
  • Notifications: To show prayer time reminders
  • Host permissions for canterburymosque.co.uk: To fetch prayer times

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
